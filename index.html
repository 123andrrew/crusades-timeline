The crusades are military expeditions to the Holyland known as Jerusalem and the land was currently owned by the Muslims. The crusaders were fighting against the Muslims to reconquer the Holy land for the Christians in Europe.

Most of the time, crusaders did not care about taking back the holy land but instead cared about gaining wealth or being forgiven of their sins.
